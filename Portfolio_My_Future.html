My Future


This is how I see my future for the moment : Ideally, I dream of becoming a full-time high-level athlete in sailing. However, I recognize the importance of being realistic. Therefore, my more attainable goal is to work as a captain on large boats, preferably on sailing yachts, with a schedule of six months on and six months off. During my 6 month off, I envision myself exploring the world on a sailing boat or/and participating in regattas.


To achieve this ambition, I intend to enroll in the Maritime School in Auckland, a city renowned as a hub for sailing. There, I aim to obtain a “Diploma in Foreign-going Master or Chief (Level 7)”. This qualification will equip me with the necessary skills and knowledge to pursue my career aspirations in the maritime industry.
